The Best Movies According to Ciel

My Top 3 Movies Are...

Cool Runnings

This is based on a true story of a Jamaican Bobsled team that competed in the Winter Olympics. A heart-warming and funny movie that the whole family will love.

Frozen

A unique fairytale version of the Snow Queen that will delight everyone in the family.

National Treasure

An action packed movie full of mysteries. A must see for all treasure hunters.
